What does LurkerLogs do?

LurkerLogs is a bot that lurks in your channel and records your viewers chat messages.
On this website, you can view these and compare them to other streamers and get some insight in how they behave across the platform. Besides logging users, we also keep track of viewer watch time and other statistics for viewers to enjoy.
